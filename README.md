## Breach Alert - Hunt Down Compromised Passwords

BreachAlert is a powerful and intuitive command-line tool designed to help users safeguard their online accounts. With just a few simple commands, you can check if your passwords have been involved in known data breaches, all from the comfort of your computer terminal. By leveraging secure hashing algorithms and reputable breach databases, BreachAlert ensures your password privacy remains intact while providing real-time insights into potential vulnerabilities. Say goodbye to the worry of compromised credentials and take control of your online security with BreachAlert!
